solar Company

Tejogreg's Ventures is a renewable energy company that brings alternative power to homes and businesses.The problem of electricity has been an age long problem in Nigeria with no hope of any improvement.This lack of power reduces optimum performance of so many businesses. Hence,the CEO,Engr.Tetsola Tuoyo(a COREN and NSE certified engineer) took it upon himself to set up this company which has provided power to many homes and businesses.

Renewable enery is the future of the world seeing that the conventional methods of generating power are very expensive and exhaustible. The incessant maintenance of the generating power plants and generators make using conventional methods more tedious and costly as opposed to the renewable energy techniques that require little maintenance or no maintenance. Installing a solar powered system only requires inital associated cost. There is no buying of fuel everyday for this system to function.
